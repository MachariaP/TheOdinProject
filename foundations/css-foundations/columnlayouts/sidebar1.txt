<h2>Iconic Game Moments</h2>
<p>Some retro games gave us moments we’ll never forget. Think of Mario’s first jump over a Goomba in Super Mario Bros., or Link finding the Master Sword in Zelda. These scenes hooked us and defined gaming’s golden age.</p>
<h3>Best Boss Fights</h3>
<p>Bowser’s fiery showdowns in Super Mario Bros. 3 set the bar high. Simple, yet epic—those battles kept us coming back.</p>
<h3>Hidden Gems</h3>
<p>EarthBound on the SNES brought quirky humor to RPGs. It’s a cult classic that still inspires fans and creators today.</p>
