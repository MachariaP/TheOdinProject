Beyond the Controller

Video game characters are more than just pixels on a screen—they’ve got skills you’d never expect. Take Mario, who’s not just jumping on Goombas but fixing pipes like a pro. What about Lara Croft? She’s raiding tombs and uncovering ancient secrets with a knack for archaeology. These hidden talents make them legends in and out of the game world. Who knew digital heroes could multitask like this?

Leveling Up in Real Life

Sonic isn’t just fast on his feet—he’s whipping up chili dogs in record time, proving speed isn’t just for running. Then there’s Link, who can juggle swords while saving Hyrule, a talent that’s as impressive as it is dangerous. From Pac-Man tending a maze-shaped garden to Kratos teaching anger management (with mixed results), these characters show there’s more to them than meets the eye. Game over? Not quite—they’re just getting started.
